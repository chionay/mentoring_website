About Us

PeerMentor Hub is a student-to-student mentoring and tutoring platform. It connects foundation and bachelor’s degree students for academic support and community-building.

We match level 4 students with experienced level 5+ students for effective guidance. Mentors can offer their time for free (to build their CV) or charge up to £50/hour.

Mentor profiles highlight their completed units, skills, pricing, and contact info so mentees can choose the right match.

This platform isn’t just tutoring — it’s a growing community built on support and shared success.

“I was struggling with my first year — until I found PeerMentor Hub. Now, I’m not only succeeding but also helping others.”

Let’s make academic life easier — together.
