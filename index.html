About Us

PeerMentor Hub is a student-to-student mentoring and tutoring platform created to empower university learners. It connects students from foundation and bachelor’s degree levels for academic growth and community-building.

Whether you're just beginning or already advancing in your course, we match level 4 students with experienced level 5+ students for effective, peer-led guidance.

Mentors have the flexibility to offer their time voluntarily (building their CV) or charge up to £50/hour, making this platform both rewarding and practical.

Mentor profiles highlight completed units, skills, pricing, and direct contact details — allowing mentees to choose a match that suits them best.

This is more than tutoring — it’s a growing community built on support, success, and shared student experiences.

“I was struggling with my first year — until I found PeerMentor Hub. Now, not only am I passing with confidence, but I’m also helping others.” – Student Testimonial

Whether you’re seeking help or ready to offer it, PeerMentor Hub is your go-to space to connect, learn, and grow. Let’s make academic life easier — together.
