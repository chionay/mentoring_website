About Us

PeerMentor Hub is a student-to-student mentoring and tutoring platform created to empower university learners.

We connect students from foundation and bachelor’s degree levels for academic growth and community-building.

Level 5+ students can offer guidance or earn money up to £50/hour — or volunteer to build their CV.

Mentor profiles show units completed, price, skills and contact details.

“I was struggling with my first year — until I found PeerMentor Hub. Now, not only am I passing with confidence, but I’m also helping others.”

Let’s make academic life easier — together.
